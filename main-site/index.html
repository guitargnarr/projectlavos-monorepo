Development Methodology

Four core principles guide all work:

  1. Substance first - Working software over impressive pitches.
  2. Built to last - Good systems outlast good intentions.
  3. Measure twice - Test the work, then ship it.
  4. People over tech - Technology works for humans, not the reverse.

Additional principles: "Show, don't tell" (working software over impressive pitches), "Simple scales" (complexity is easy, clarity takes work), "Test the seams" (systems fail where they connect), "Tools serve people" (technology works for humans, not the reverse).

Guitar Reference Library (projectlavos.com/guitar)

24 guitar scale reference books available as downloadable PDFs. Each book covers 12 keys, 5 CAGED positions, 8 exercise types, and 480 total exercises with mathematically verified tablature. All tablature is generated by computational fretboard logic, not hand-transcribed.

Scale categories and books:

  • Diatonic Modes (7): Natural Major (Ionian), Natural Minor (Aeolian), Dorian, Phrygian, Lydian, Mixolydian, Locrian
  • Harmonic Minor Family (2): Harmonic Minor, Phrygian Dominant
  • Melodic Minor Family (3): Melodic Minor (Jazz Minor), Lydian Dominant, Altered
  • Symmetric Scales (3): Whole Tone, Half-Whole Diminished, Whole-Half Diminished

  • Exotic Scales (4): Double Harmonic (Byzantine/Arabic), Hungarian Minor, Hirajoshi (Japanese), In Sen (Japanese)

Total: 24 books x 480 exercises = 11,520 exercises across all scales. This library connects to the FretVision guitar learning platform at guitar.projectlavos.com.
